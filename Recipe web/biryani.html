Chicken Biryani

Type :- Non-Vegetarian

Style :- South Indian

Cooking Time :- 60 minutes

Best Served With :- Mosru baggi(Raita), Sherva(sambar)

Varieties :- Chicken Dum Biryani, Mutton Biryani etc.

Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish cooked with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and rice.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup rice.
  2. 500g chicken pieces.
  3. 1 cup curd.
  4. 2 onions, thinly sliced.
  5. 2 tomatoes, chopped.
  6. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste.
  7. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder.
  8. 2 teaspoons biryani masala.
  9. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder.
  10. 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint and coriander leaves.
  11. 2 tablespoons ghee or oil.
  12. Salt to taste.
  13. Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional).
  14. Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamom, cloves).

Procedure

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% done. Drain and keep aside.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Fry onions until golden brown and crispy. Remove half of the onions for garnishing.
  4. In the same pan, add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's nearly done.
  6. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle fresh mint, coriander leaves, and fried onions on top.
  7. Drizzle saffron milk over the rice.
  8. Cover the pan with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-30 minutes until the rice and chicken are fully cooked.
  9. Serve hot with mosru baggi(raita) or sherva(sambar).

Serve With

  1. Mosru baggi(raita).
  2. Sherva(Sambar).
  3. Round chopped onions.
  4. Round chopped cucumber.
  5. Small amount of lime juice.

Variants

  1. Chicken Dum Biryani.
  2. Mutton Biryani :- Same as chicken biryani, but instead pf chicken add mutton.
