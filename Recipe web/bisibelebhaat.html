Bisi Bele Bhaat

Type :- Vegetarian

Style :- South Indian

Cooking Time :- 30 minutes

Best Served With :- Khara boondi(Mirchi boondi), Ghee

Varieties :- Add cashews, peanuts, or fried onions for extra texture and flavor.

Description

Bisi Bele Bhaat is a traditional Karnataka dish made from rice, lentils, vegetables, and a special spice blend. It's a hearty, flavorful one-pot meal.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup rice.
  2. 1/2 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas).
  3. 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, etc).
  4. 1/2 cup tamarind extract.
  5. 2 tablespoons Bisi Bele Bhaat powder.
  6. 2 tablespoons ghee.
  7. 1 teaspoon mustard seeds.
  8. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
  9. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
  10. A pinch of asafoetida.
  11. Curry leaves.
  12. Salt to taste.

Procedure

  1. Cook rice and toor dal separately until soft.
  2. In a pot, heat ghee and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and saut√© for a few minutes.
  4. Add tamarind extract, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add cooked rice and dal to the pot. Mix well.
  6. Add Bisi Bele Bhaat powder and adjust the consistency with water if needed.
  7. Simmer for a few minutes until the flavors meld together.
  8. Serve hot with a drizzle of ghee.

Serve With

  1. Khara boondi(Mirchi boondi).
  2. Ghee.

Variants

  1. Add cashews, peanuts, or fried onions for extra texture and flavor.
