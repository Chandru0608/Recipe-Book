Fish Fry

Type :- Non-Vegetarian

Style :- South Indian

Cooking Time :- 60 minutes

Best Served With :- Round chooped Onions, Cucumber and small amount of lime juice

Varieties :- Amritsari Fish Fry, Andhra Fish Vepudu, Fish Fingers.

Description

Fish Fry is a crispy and spicy dish made with fish fillets or pieces that are marinated in a flavorful spice mix and then fried until golden brown. It is a popular appetizer or side dish that pairs well with rice, bread, or salads.

Ingredients

  1. 500 gms fish fillets or pieces (tilapia, pomfret, or kingfish).
  2. 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste.
  3. 1 tbsp lemon juice.
  4. 1 tsp turmeric powder.
  5. 1 tsp red chili powder.
  6. 1 tsp coriander powder.
  7. 1 tsp garam masala.
  8. Salt to taste.
  9. 2 tbsp rice flour or gram flour (for coating).
  10. Oil for frying.

Procedure

  1. Marinate the Fish :- Clean and wash the fish pieces thoroughly. Pat them dry with a kitchen towel. In a bowl, mix the ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Coat the fish pieces with this marinade and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Coat with Flour :- After marinating, coat the fish pieces with rice flour or gram flour on both sides.
  3. Fry the Fish :- Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the coated fish pieces until they are golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  4. Serve :- Serve hot with lemon wedges, onion rings, and mint or tamarind chutney.

Serve With

  1. Round chooped onions.
  2. Round chooped cucumber.
  3. Smal amount of lime juice.

Variants

  1. Amritsari Fish Fry :- Fish marinated with spices and coated with a batter of chickpea flour, then deep-fried.
  2. Andhra Fish Vepudu :- Fish marinated with spices and shallow-fried until crispy.
  3. Fish Fingers :- Fish pieces coated with breadcrumbs and deep-fried.
