Chicken Kabab

Type :- Non-Vegetarian

Style :- South Indian

Cooking Time :- 60 minutes

Best Served With :- Round chooped Onions, Cucumber and small amount of lime juice

Varieties :- Shami Kabab, Seekh Kabab, Galouti Kabab etc.

Description

Chicken Kabab is a popular dish made with marinated chicken pieces that are grilled or fried to perfection. The kababs are juicy, flavorful, and can be served as an appetizer or a main course with rice, bread, or salads.

Ingredients

  1. 500 gms boneless chicken (cut into cubes).
  2. 2 eggs.
  3. 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Green chilli paste.
  5. 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped).
  6. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
  7. 1 tsp garam masala.
  8. 1 tsp cumin powder.
  9. Salt to taste.

Procedure

  1. Marinate the Chicken :- In a bowl, mix the chicken cubes with 2 raw eggs, ginger-garlic paste, green chili paste, coriander leaves, red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, cumin powder, salt. Mix well and leave it for at least 1 hour.
  2. Fill half pan with oil and pre heat it.
  3. After the oil is heated well, put the marinated mixed chicken cubes one by one.
  4. Let them to be deep fried or cooked in medium flame for 10 - 15 minutes.
  5. Now take the cubes out if the chicken inside is cooked well. Now the kabab is ready to eat.

Serve With

  1. Round chooped onions.
  2. Round chooped cucumber.
  3. Smal amount of lime juice.

Variants

  1. Shami Kabab :- Minced chicken mixed with soaked and ground chana dal, spices, and cooked on a tawa.
  2. Seekh Kabab :- Minced chicken mixed with spices and shaped around skewers, grilled or fried.
  3. Galouti Kabab :- Minced chicken mixed with spices and cooked on a tawa, known for its melt-in-your-mouth texture.
