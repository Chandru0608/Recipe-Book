Puliogare/Tamarind Rice

Type :- Vegetarian

Style :- South Indian

Cooking Time :- 30 minutes

Best Served With :- Original

Varieties :- Add grated coconut, sesame seeds, or fried onions for a different twist.

Description

Puliogare is a tangy and spicy tamarind rice dish from South India, often served during festivals and special occasions.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups cooked rice.
  2. 1/2 cup tamarind extract.
  3. 2 tablespoons oil.
  4. 1 teaspoon mustard seeds.
  5. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
  6. 2-3 dried red chilies.
  7. A pinch of asafoetida.
  8. Curry leaves.
  9. 1/4 cup roasted peanuts.
  10. 2 tablespoons Puliogare powder.
  11. Salt to taste.

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves.
  2. Add tamarind extract and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add Puliogare powder and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add roasted peanuts and cooked rice. Mix until the rice is well-coated with the tamarind mixture.
  5. Serve at room temperature or slightly warm.

Variants

  1. Add grated coconut, sesame seeds, or fried onions for a different twist.
